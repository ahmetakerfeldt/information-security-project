Key Assessments
  • is_disposable_email: Indicates the email is not disposable. Disposable emails are often used in phishing attacks. A value of "false" suggests the email might be more reliable.
  • is_free_email: Shows the email is from a free provider (e.g., Gmail, Yahoo). Free email services are frequently used in phishing but are not a definitive indicator of a threat.
  • is_role_email: Indicates the email is not role-based. Role-based emails are commonly used in official communications but can also be utilized in phishing.
  • is_catchall_email: Indicates the email is not a catch-all email (accepting all emails on the domain). This increases the likelihood of it being a real userâ€™s email.

  • quality_score: A high-quality score indicates better email reliability. Low scores are typical in phishing emails, but a high score does not eliminate the risk entirely.
